# Code of Conduct

This project is for rigorous technical work. Treat contributors with respect,
argue from evidence, and keep discussion focused on the code, docs, or product
claim under review.

## Expected behavior

- Be precise and constructive.
- Separate facts, assumptions, and opinions.
- Critique ideas directly without personal attacks.
- Help newcomers find the relevant file, command, or issue.
- Respect maintainer decisions on scope and roadmap.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Repeated off-topic arguments after maintainers ask to stop.
- Publishing private information or secrets.
- Deliberately submitting malicious code, obfuscated payloads, or misleading
  evidence.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or delete comments; close issues; block users; or
reject contributions that violate this code. Security-sensitive reports should
follow `SECURITY.md`.
